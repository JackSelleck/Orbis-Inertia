Ethan. R. Holmes

Foley Artist & Sound Designer

2025 Ethan Holmes

I am the Foley artist, responsible for recording, editing and implementing sounds. Each sound I created is used across the environment, as I've tried to make sure that everything has a designated sound.

Throughout this project, my job & role was to edit and implement the sounds. Id attempt to make/ edit each sound with my peers feedback and the overall asset in mind. When implementing, I needed to write a breif amount of code to make sure each sound played correctly.

I believe that I have done a lot of adujsting to my implementations, as I've tried to make sure that they fit into the game, whilst also fitting into my teammates and play testers expectations.

During this process, I have enjoyed my time working on this, as I've spent time working on a role that I like, with my peers, and pick up on a few additional skills along the way, which I feel is super helpufl & inshightful. However, I feel as though I could've created more high quality sounds, as I feel this would have enhanced the game even more.
